Invulnerable Vagrant

Nestled between a taxidermist and a butchers shop on Zadash' busy Penta Market is a two story building of dark stained wood with green and gold velvet hanging on the outside as a small overhang. Slits of glass on the doors and the heavy stained glass in the windows reveal a warm lit interior. Above the doors in a carefully carved plaque written in common and elvish is the name of this shop: "The invulnerable vagrant"

The interior is filled with large cabinets that are filled with items. A large desk rests a few feet from the entryway. Behind which sits a large looming figure covered in blue fur. The figure has a curly mop of brown hair. On his wide nose sits a pair of spectacles that are comparatively tiny. In a soft-spoken and mellow voice he greets you; "Oh, hi there! Welcome to the Invulnerable Vagrant! You all look like just the type of client we're used to welcoming here! My name is Pumat Sol".

Peeking from behind one of the cabinets are two copies the exact same figure. Magical simulacrum that help Pumat run his (or their) store. "Let me know if you need any help with your selection of our products!" they say in unison.
