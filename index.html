üé≤ What do I need?

If you've never played D&D before, or you have little experience creating characters, all you need is a D&DBeyond account, which is free. And you need a way to roll dice. I recommend this free dice-rolling app on android and this free dice-rolling app on iOS.

You will also need a Google account and a mic on your laptop to play over Google Hangouts.

If you're a bit more experienced, you can create your own character in D&DBeyond. Once the date nears for our session, I'll contact you with a link to add your character to the group.

D&D is all about the character you play. But for new players, creating a character is pretty rough. If you're experienced: great! let me know what you plan on playing.

For the new players, I've created a couple of characters for you. These are ready to go and each one will get their moment to shine in these adventures.

  • Human Paladin.

    Knight-in-shinging armor type. Can fight and heal people.

  • Changeling Rogue.

    Sneaky shapeshifter who is a great scout and infiltrator.

  • Half-elf Bard.

    Inspiring magic user that boasts their allies in combat and is good in almost every skill.

  • Tieffling Warlock.

    Tiefflings are humanoids with devil-blood running through their veins. This one made a pact with a very evil being granting them magical powers.

  • Human Wizard.

    Think Gandalf, add a pinch of Doctor Strange.

    Difficulty: intermediate
  • Goliath Barbarian.

    The best solution to every problem is a greataxe. Can take and deal damage like a boss, but is a bit stupid.

  • Dwarven Cleric.

    Stoic healer and excellent fighter.

  • Elven Ranger.

    Can survive in the wild like a pro and has excellent combat skills.
